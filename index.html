ABOUT MYSELF

Who I Am?

I am Muhammad Umer, a Game Designer and Project Coordinator from Rawalpindi, Pakistan, with experience in game designing since 2021, My expertise lies in project coordination, game design, and utilizing Unity engine (beginner level) for level creation. I have worked at Spartans Global Pvt. Ltd. as a QA Engineer than at Mobify, where I started as a Game Designer and later promoted to Project Coordinator. I have designed games in a variety of genres, including simulation, puzzle, casual, role-playing, with a personal passion for Single-Player games. I am focused on creating exciting experiences, using my technical and management skills in every project.
